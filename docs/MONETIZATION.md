### 2. FATI Token Sales (Secondary Revenue)

**Conversion Rate:** 1 USD = 100 FATI

**Average Purchase:** $15 per user per month
**Target:** 30% of active users purchase tokens monthly

| Users | Purchase Rate | Avg Purchase | Monthly Revenue |
|-------|--------------|--------------|-----------------|
| 1,000 | 30% | $15 | $4,500 |

### 3. Marketplace Commissions

**10% commission on all marketplace transactions**

Premium Items:
- Consulta Profunda: 500 FATI ($5) ‚Üí $0.50 commission
- Avatar Personalizado: 1,000 FATI ($10) ‚Üí $1.00 commission
- Pack de Voz Premium: 300 FATI ($3) ‚Üí $0.30 commission

**Target:** 200 marketplace transactions/month ‚Üí $1,000

### 4. Referral Program (User Acquisition)

**Cost-effective growth mechanism:**
- Referrer: 20% of first purchase in FATI (cost ~$2-5 per acquisition)
- Referee: 100 FATI welcome bonus (~$1 cost)
- **Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC):** $3-6 per user
- **Lifetime Value (LTV):** $180-360 (assuming 6-12 month retention)
- **LTV:CAC Ratio:** 30:1 to 60:1 (excellent)

### 5. Enterprise Solutions

**Custom Features:**
- White-label deployment: $500-2,000/month
- Custom integrations: $1,000-5,000 one-time
- Dedicated support: $500/month add-on
- Custom avatars: $250 per avatar

- Stripe fees: 2.9% + $0.30 per transaction
- Domain + SSL: $15/year

**Variable Costs:**
- Gemini API usage: ~$0.50 per 1M tokens
- Database costs: Scale with users
- Referral bonuses: Paid in FATI (virtual currency)

**Total Monthly Fixed Costs:** ~$50-100

## üéØ Growth Strategy

### Phase 1: Launch (Month 1-2)
- **Goal:** 100 users, $500 MRR
- **Tactics:**
  - Beta launch with early adopter discount (50% off first month)
  - Product Hunt launch
  - Social media campaign (Twitter, LinkedIn)
  - Content marketing (blog posts, tutorials)

### Phase 2: Scaling (Month 3-6)
- **Goal:** 1,000 users, $10,000 MRR
- **Tactics:**
  - Referral program activation
  - Partnerships with AI/tech communities
  - Guest posting on relevant blogs
  - YouTube demos and tutorials
  - Case studies from early users

### Phase 3: Optimization (Month 7-12)
- **Goal:** 5,000 users, $50,000 MRR
- **Tactics:**
  - Paid advertising (Google Ads, Facebook)
  - Influencer partnerships
  - Webinar series
  - Enterprise outreach
  - Feature expansion based on feedback

## üí° Upsell Strategy

### Free ‚Üí Basic
- Trigger: After 10 daily queries limit hit 3 times
- Offer: "Unlock unlimited queries for $9.99/month"

### Basic ‚Üí Pro
- Trigger: User tries voice feature or advanced analytics
- Offer: "Upgrade to Pro for full voice access + analytics"

### Pro ‚Üí Enterprise
- Trigger: API usage patterns or team size >5
